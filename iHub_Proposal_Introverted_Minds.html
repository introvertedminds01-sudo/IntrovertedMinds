Scope of Work

What we're building.

Based on your requirements, the scope covers the following key workstreams — each scoped at the depth required to deliver a complete, production-ready platform across all four markets.

01 — Core Deliverable
Multilingual Country Architecture
Subfolder-based internationalisation across 4 markets: Singapore (/sg/), Malaysia (/my/), Hong Kong (/hk/ — Chinese Traditional), Thailand (/th/ — Thai). Each locale includes country-specific routing, localised CTAs, contact flows, and language-specific SEO with hreflang tags and individual XML sitemaps per country.
02 — Core Deliverable
Custom UI/UX Design
Full custom interface design for all 12 main pages — no templates, no off-the-shelf themes. Design reflects iHub's LogTech positioning: premium, technology-forward, and trustworthy across diverse cultural contexts. Mobile-first with full responsive design across all viewports.
03 — Core Deliverable
React (Next.js) Development
All 12 main pages built in React using Next.js for server-side rendering — critical for SEO performance across all 4 locales. Component-based architecture for scalability and long-term maintainability. Clean, documented codebase with full knowledge transfer upon project completion.
04 — Core Deliverable
CMS for Blog & Articles
A content management system enabling iHub's marketing team to publish and manage blog articles, news, and insights without developer involvement. Optimal CMS solution — Webflow, Sanity, or equivalent — confirmed during our discovery call based on your team's preference.
05 — Core Deliverable
Country-Specific Forms & CTAs
Dedicated enquiry forms per locale routing to the correct country team. Transaction buttons configured per market with country-specific contact details and messaging. PDPA-compliant data consent handling for the Singapore locale.
06 — Core Deliverable
Google Tools & Marketing Integrations
Google Analytics 4 with country/locale segmentation, Google Tag Manager, Google Search Console per locale, conversion tracking across all country-specific forms, and integration with additional marketing tools identified during discovery.

Technical Architecture

How we're building it.

Every technical decision is chosen for performance, scalability, and long-term maintainability — with a direct business justification behind each choice.

Frontend Framework
Next.js (React)
Upgrading from create-react-app to Next.js delivers server-side rendering — critical for SEO across all 4 locales. Built-in i18n routing handles the subfolder structure natively, preserving your existing React investment while dramatically improving search performance.
Internationalisation
next-i18next
Industry-standard i18n library for Next.js. Handles locale routing, language detection, translation file management, and script-specific rendering for Chinese Traditional and Thai. Custom font loading per locale ensures perfect typographic rendering.
Content Management
Headless CMS
We recommend evaluating Sanity or Webflow CMS during discovery. Both provide non-technical editors a visual interface for blog and article management. Final recommendation based on your team's comfort. API-connected to Next.js for seamless content delivery.
SEO Infrastructure
Multilingual SEO
Hreflang tags across all locales. Individual XML sitemaps per country subfolder submitted to Google Search Console. Locale-specific meta tags and OG data. Structured data (Schema.org) for B2B service and LogTech discovery.
Analytics & Tracking
Google Tag Manager
GA4 with locale segmentation, GTM container setup, Search Console per property, conversion tracking for all country-specific forms, LinkedIn Insight Tag, and any additional integrations confirmed in discovery.
Hosting & Infrastructure
Cloud Deployment
Vercel or AWS Amplify — both provide global CDN with strong APAC edge nodes (Singapore, Hong Kong, Mumbai) for fast load times across all target markets. SSL, automatic deployments, and environment management included.
Architecture Note: The React + CMS hybrid will be finalised during discovery. We will advise on the most practical integration based on your team's capabilities and long-term content goals. No architectural assumptions are locked in — this is a recommendation, not a prescription.

Complimentary Website Audit

ihubsolutions.com — The honest picture.

As part of our commitment to this partnership, we conducted a complimentary audit of ihubsolutions.com prior to this proposal. We examined your site's architecture, content structure, multilingual readiness, conversion design, and technical foundation. Here is what we found.

Overall Website Health Score
47/100
Needs Significant Improvement
The site's technical foundation (React) is solid and the content quality is genuine. However, multiple critical structural, UX, and multilingual gaps are actively limiting iHub's ability to convert international visitors — and presenting a brand image that lags behind iHub's real stature.
Multilingual Architecture
Critical
The site is entirely Singapore-centric with zero multilingual infrastructure. Every contact form states: "Please note that this form submits your interest for services in Singapore only." Your Malaysia, Hong Kong, and Thailand offices have no functional web presence — directly costing you regional leads every day.
Hong Kong Market Page
Critical
Your HK presence lives at /promo/hk-warehouse/ — a 2017 promotional page never completed. The footer contains unedited placeholder text: "This is the bottom slider area. You can edit this text." Any HK prospect discovering this page would immediately lose confidence in iHub as a professional logistics partner.
URL Architecture
Critical
The Contact page sits at /promo/contact-us/ — a non-standard URL implying a temporary campaign page, not a core company page. This affects SEO credibility, user trust, and future link-building efforts.
Brand & Visual Design
Needs Attention
The current design does not reflect the calibre of a company visited by Singapore ministers and awarded the Singapore Quality Class with Innovation. The visual language is conservative and dated relative to iHub's positioning as a LogTech innovator deploying AI, ML, IoT, and proprietary digital systems.
Content & SEO Engine
Needs Attention
There is no blog or content section on the current site. For a company with 25 years of operational knowledge across 15 industry verticals, the absence of a content strategy means iHub is essentially invisible in organic search for high-intent logistics queries across all 4 markets.
Conversion Design
Needs Attention
No country-specific conversion paths, no differentiated CTAs per service, no lead qualification flow. A healthcare prospect and an AI/ML prospect receive the same generic "Contact Us" path. For a B2B company serving 200+ clients across different verticals and geographies, this is a lost conversion at every single touchpoint.
React Frontend Foundation
Solid
The site is built on React (create-react-app) — a strong technical choice we will build upon. The existing React foundation means the revamp can leverage current infrastructure while upgrading to Next.js for SSR, better SEO, and native multilingual routing.
Content Quality
Strong
The copy across service pages is well-written, specific, and reflects genuine operational depth. The brand personality — confident and refreshingly human — is a real differentiator. This tone will be preserved and elevated in the redesign, not genericised.
Audit Note: This audit was conducted using publicly available information, site crawl data, and UX analysis. A comprehensive technical audit covering Core Web Vitals, full backlink profile, and complete SEO health assessment will be conducted during the project discovery phase at no additional cost.
